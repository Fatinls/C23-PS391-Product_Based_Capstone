The weather dataset consists of two types:
 the one is for the weather classification;
 and the other on is a time series data to predict temperature, humidity, and other data that helps to classify weather.

The source of the time series data is from: https://dataonline.bmkg.go.id/data_iklim
The source of the classification data is from: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/muthuj7/weather-dataset
